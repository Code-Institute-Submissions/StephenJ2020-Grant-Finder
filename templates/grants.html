Grant Finder

This site is intended for use by those living or working in the North Kildare area.

If you have ever applied for a Grant before you will know how time consuming the process can be and even more so when you have to search multiple sources to get all the Information you require. This aim of Grand Finder is to bring all of this information together in one central point. The site is Free to use but you will need to register so that you can view the full details or have access to upload details of any Grants that you are aware of and wish to share with other users.
